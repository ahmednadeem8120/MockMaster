Job Title: Delivery Driver
Company: SwiftMove Logistics
Location: Dubai, UAE
Employment Type: Full-Time

About the Role
SwiftMove Logistics is looking for a reliable and experienced Delivery Driver to join our growing fleet operations team in Dubai. You will be responsible for the safe and timely delivery of parcels, documents, and food orders to residential and commercial customers across Dubai and surrounding emirates. This is a customer-facing role and we expect a high standard of professionalism at all times.

Key Responsibilities
- Collect parcels and packages from the warehouse or depot and deliver them to customer addresses accurately and on time.
- Plan and follow efficient delivery routes using Google Maps or company navigation tools to minimise travel time and fuel usage.
- Handle cash on delivery transactions and submit accurate daily collection reports to the supervisor.
- Obtain proof of delivery from customers through signatures or digital confirmation on the delivery app.
- Conduct daily pre-trip vehicle inspections and report any defects, damage, or maintenance needs immediately.
- Load and unload vehicles carefully to prevent damage to cargo and ensure items are secured during transit.
- Communicate professionally with customers via phone to confirm delivery times, resolve address queries, and manage failed delivery attempts.
- Maintain accurate delivery logs and update the tracking system in real time.
- Adhere to UAE traffic laws and company road safety policies at all times.
- Assist with parcel sorting and inventory checks at the depot when required.

Requirements
- Valid UAE Light Vehicle Driving Licence — Category 3 or equivalent.
- Minimum 2 years of professional driving experience in the UAE.
- Clean driving record with no major traffic violations.
- Familiarity with Dubai road network and surrounding emirates.
- Basic smartphone and navigation app proficiency — Google Maps, delivery tracking apps.
- Ability to handle cash transactions accurately and responsibly.
- Good communication skills in English — Arabic is an advantage.
- Physically fit to load and unload packages throughout the shift.
- Punctual, reliable, and able to work under time pressure.

Nice to Have
- Prior experience with logistics companies such as Aramex, Noon, Fetchr, or similar.
- Defensive driving certificate or road safety training.
- Food handling certificate for food delivery roles.
- Experience using fleet management or delivery management software.

What We Offer
- Competitive monthly salary plus delivery incentives.
- Company vehicle and fuel card provided.
- Medical insurance.
- Paid annual leave as per UAE Labour Law.
- Opportunities for promotion to senior driver or logistics coordinator roles.
